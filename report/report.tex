\begin{abstract}
We present a proof-of-concept multi-expert agentic pipeline for \textit{in silico}
peptide generation~\cite{wang2022machine}. A central orchestrator coordinates
three specialized agents: an AI Designer, a Chemistry Constraints agent, and a
Biology agent providing an embedding-based ranking signal~\cite{veltri2018deep}.
The generator is based on variational architectures (VAE/CVAE), and chemistry and biology
scores are fused by the orchestrator to build a global Top-$K$ ranking across iterations.
\par\vspace{0.5\baselineskip}
\noindent\textbf{Keywords:} Multi-Agent Systems, Peptide Generation,
In Silico Drug Design, VAE/CVAE, Evolutionary Scale Modeling.
\end{abstract}
